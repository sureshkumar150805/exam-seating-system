How the Algorithm Works
Anti-Cheating Measures:
  • Bench Types: Different seating patterns per bench
  • Year Separation: Same year students not on same bench
  • Pattern Rotation: Different patterns per room
  • Distribution Strategies: Even student spread
Distribution Strategies:
  • Block: Group students by year across rooms
  • Round Robin: Distribute evenly across rooms
  • Seeded Shuffle: Shuffle within years, then distribute
